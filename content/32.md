Cisco enumerates your system.

<!-- more -->
   
`CiscoJabberPrt.exe` will pipe `ipconfig.exe /all`, `systeminfo.exe`, and `tasklist.exe` into a file named `Systeminfo.txt` inside of the User's `%TEMP%` folder.
